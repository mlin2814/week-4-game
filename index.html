Crystal Gem Training Simulation

Do You Wish to Engage in Combat?

In this RPG, you will train to protect Earth from Homeworld! The Gems will train with each other to make sure they're ready for Homeworld Gems. You will select one Crystal Gem as your player, and the others will be your sparring partners. Each Gem has a certain amount of attack power and HP. Your goal is to beat the other Gems and come out victorious! Your attacks get progressively stronger, but your HP will decrease with every blow. Don't worry, you'll be able to regenerate if your gem takes too much damage. Except for Steven. No one knows what will happen, and that's why he'll be watching on the sidelines with Connie and Greg. Sorry.

Let the training commence!
